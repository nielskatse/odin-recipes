Great Cinnamon Toast

Toast tastes great like bread!

Ingredients

  • 2 slices white bread
  • 2 teaspoons butter or margarine
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Steps

  1. Toast bread in toaster
  2. Spread butter or margarine on each slice
  3. Stir together sugar and cinnamon in small bowl
  4. Sprinkle generously over hot toast
  5. Eat bread
